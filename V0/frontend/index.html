🔒 Privacy Explanation

✓ PRIVATE (encrypted on-chain):

  • Individual vote choice of each voter
  • Voter-to-vote association (no link between address and vote on-chain)
  • Intermediate tallies during voting

✗ PUBLIC (visible on-chain):

  • Election existence and title
  • Candidates/options list
  • Final aggregated results (after decryption)
  • Number of voters who participated
  • Timing of election close
